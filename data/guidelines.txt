- Any roof older than 20 years is considered high-risk and is an automatic rejection.
- Visible foundation cracks, regardless of size, require a mandatory secondary inspection and flag the property for REVIEW.
- Properties with knob-and-tube or aluminum wiring are ineligible for coverage and must be REJECTED.
- Properties with updated PEX or Copper plumbing are considered low-risk in this category and are a positive factor.
- Water damage indicated by active leaks or significant staining is grounds for rejection until repaired.
